About Me

I am an enthusiastic Information Technology student with a strong focus on front-end development and database management. My journey into technology began with a simple curiosity about how websites function, which has grown into a passion for creating clean, efficient code and user-friendly designs. I enjoy turning ideas into interactive digital experiences and continuously strive to improve my skills while exploring new technologies.

Educational Background

Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT). I'm focused on grasping the core principles of computer science while actively experimenting with modern web technologies outside the classroom. I believe in learning by doing, which drives my project-based approach.

Technical Interests

  • Exploring serverless architectures
  • Understanding data structures and algorithms
  • Modern JavaScript frameworks and libraries
  • Database optimization and query performance

Hobbies & Interests

  • Hiking and outdoor adventures
  • Playing competitive chess (to hone problem-solving skills)
  • Contributing to small open-source projects
  • Reading tech blogs and staying updated with industry trends
  • Photography and digital art
